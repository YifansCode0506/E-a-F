About Us

We are a comprehensive institution dedicated to family services and personalized educational support. Whether it is meticulous household care or tailored companion-style teaching, we always uphold the principle of “wholehearted service and warm companionship”, providing every family with reliable, professional, and heartfelt care.

We firmly believe that every service should be built on trust, respect, and responsibility. Therefore, we not only focus on service quality but also value genuine emotional connections between people.

With patience, we attend to every detail; with professionalism, we safeguard every trust. We look forward to becoming a reliable assistant in your daily life and a warm companion in your child's journey of growth.

The Insisting Concepts

Education Concepts

  • Tailored teaching to inspire potential: Every child has a unique pace and learning style. We are committed to discovering and nurturing each child's potential by creating personalized learning plans.

  • Cultivating independent learning skills: Education is not only about imparting knowledge but also about inspiring ways of thinking. We guide children to develop habits of active thinking and independent learning.

  • Building trust through companionship: By fostering positive interactions, we make teaching a process of warmth, trust, and constructive growth.

  • Supporting holistic child development: We care not only about academic performance but also about mental health, social skills, and lifestyle habits, offering well-rounded support for children's growth.

Housekeeping Concepts

  • People-oriented, respecting individual differences: Every family and every client has unique needs. We listen attentively, serve with care, and respect diverse lifestyles.

  • Professional care, quality in the details: Whether it is cleaning, caregiving, or companionship, we focus on details and uphold high standards, ensuring clients truly feel safe and comfortable.

  • Warm companionship, service with a human touch: Our goal is not just to complete tasks, but to bring warmth and companionship, becoming a trustworthy part of the family.

  • Responsibility first, integrity above all: We place responsibility and integrity at the heart of our services, making sure every experience brings peace of mind and trust.

After-sales Service

Education After-sales Service

  • Post-class feedback and learning advice: After each lesson, we provide a short report highlighting the student's performance, comprehension, and areas for improvement.

  • Free Q&A time:Online consultation is available within X hours after class to help students consolidate their knowledge.

  • Flexible course adjustment:If the course pace does not suit the student, parents may request a change of teacher or a redesigned learning plan.

  • Learning review and goal setting: At the end of each stage, we provide a progress summary and set new goals, creating a continuous growth record.

Housekeeping After-sales Service

  • Quality follow-up: Within 1-2 days after service, we follow up via phone or text to assess satisfaction and identify potential issues.

  • Service correction guarantee: If the result is unsatisfactory (such as missed cleaning or process issues), a free correction or re-service will be arranged within 24 hours.

  • Liability and compensation: Clear compensation terms are in place for any losses caused during service (such as damaged items or equipment misuse).

  • Customer profile records: We maintain service records to better understand long-term client preferences and special requirements.
